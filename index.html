I am a second-year PhD student at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, advised by Prof. Shuowen ZHANG. My research focuses on Beyond-Diagonal Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (BD-RIS) in wireless communications — a next-generation architecture that breaks the constraints of conventional diagonal RIS, enabling richer wave manipulation and higher spectral efficiency for future 6G networks.

Research Interests

📡

Beyond-Diagonal RIS (BD-RIS)

Exploring advanced BD-RIS architectures that enable inter-element signal coupling, offering superior beamforming flexibility over conventional diagonal RIS for 6G systems.

📶

Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces

Designing and analyzing RIS-assisted wireless communication systems, including channel modeling, passive beamforming optimization, and performance analysis.

🔗

6G Wireless Communications

Investigating next-generation wireless network architectures and signal processing techniques to meet the demands of future ultra-reliable, high-throughput communications.
